About Me

Hello, I'm Aaron! I am a Level Designer and Technical Designer. I specialize in designing/creating levels and mechanics due to my skills in 3D modeling, background in programming and my years of experience in design. While I am specialized in these areas, I am a well balanced game developer with experience in other areas too such as production. I also have a B.S. in Computer Science: Game Design from the University of California, Santa Cruz.

My team and I recently released BALLISTIC, an online 3rd person dodgeball party game, on Steam! Currently, I am looking for a full-time position in the game industry. In the meantime, I am working on 2 games that will be released on Steam and iOS within the next 6 months. Feel free to contact me if you have any questions!
