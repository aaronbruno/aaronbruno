About Me

Hey, I'm Aaron! I'm a senior undergraduate student at UC Santa Cruz majoring in Computer Science: Game Design. I am most interested in game programming and level design but I am familiar with other aspects of game design such as 3D modeling and animation. I am currently working in a team of 9 on Ballistic, a 3rd person dodgeball party game, as a part of my senior capstone project!

This website is dedicated to showcasing some of the projects I have worked on. I am passionate about creating fun and engaging experiences for players, I always consider the player's experience and every little detail that contributes to that experience. Besides creating and playing games, I enjoy spending time with family and friends, reading books, and watching movies.

I hope you enjoy looking through my projects and learning more about me!
