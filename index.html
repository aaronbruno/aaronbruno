About Me

Hello, I'm Aaron! I'm a senior undergraduate student at UC Santa Cruz, majoring in Computer Science: Game Design. I am most interested in game programming and level design, although I'm also familiar with other aspects of game development such as 3D modeling and animation. Currently, I'm part of a team of 9 working Ballistic, a 3rd person dodgeball party game, as part of my senior capstone project.

This website is dedicated to showcasing some of the projects I have worked on. I'm passionate about creating fun and engaging experiences for players. I always strive to consider every detail that contributes to the player's experience. Besides creating and playing games, I enjoy spending time with family and friends, reading books, and watching movies.

I hope you enjoy looking through my projects and learning more about me!
