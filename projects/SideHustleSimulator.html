Side-Hustle Simulator is a project I was apart of as the UI Designer and Programmer during my junior year at UCSC. I was working with 2 others on this project over the course of 3 weeks as part of our final project for ARTG120. We decided to heavily play into the idea of being in seemingly unending debt and making body modifications and upgrades to be able to make more money.
